Community Guidelines

Everest is built to create a safe, respectful, and authentic community. By using Everest, you agree to follow these guidelines.

1. Be Respectful

Treat everyone with respect.

  • Harassment or bullying is not allowed
  • Hate speech is not allowed
  • Threats or abusive language are not allowed
  • Personal attacks are not allowed

2. No Harmful Content

Content that promotes harm is not allowed.

  • Violence
  • Terrorism
  • Dangerous activities
  • Self-harm encouragement

3. No Fake Identity

Users must not impersonate others.

  • Fake profiles pretending to be another person
  • Impersonating public figures or brands
  • Misleading identity

4. Authentic Content

Share genuine and original content.

  • No spam posts
  • No misleading or fake information
  • No mass promotion or scams

5. Respect Privacy

Respect the privacy of others.

  • Do not share private phone numbers
  • Do not share addresses
  • Do not share personal data without permission

6. No Illegal Activity

Everest does not allow content related to illegal activities.

  • Fraud
  • Selling illegal items
  • Hacking or piracy

7. Report Violations

If you see content that breaks these rules, please report it through the Report a Problem feature.

8. Enforcement

Accounts that violate these guidelines may face:

  • Content removal
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent account ban

Privacy Policy

Welcome to Everest. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Everest collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Everest, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal information such as your name, email address, and profile details.
  • Content you create such as posts, comments, photos, and messages.
  • Device and usage information including browser type, IP address, and activity on the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve Everest services.
  • Allow you to create posts and interact with users.
  • Keep the platform safe and prevent spam.
  • Improve user experience.

3. Data Protection

Everest takes appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. We use modern security practices and restricted access systems to safeguard user data. However, because the internet is not completely secure, we cannot guarantee absolute protection of all information.

4. Sharing Information

Everest does not sell or trade your personal data to third parties. Information may only be shared when necessary to operate the platform, comply with legal obligations, or protect the safety and rights of our users and services.

5. User Control

  • Edit your personal information.
  • Delete posts or content.
  • Request account deletion.

6. Cookies

Everest may use cookies to enhance site performance, remember user preferences, and improve the overall browsing experience. Disabling cookies may limit certain features of the platform.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect improvements or legal requirements. The updated version will always be published on this page with the latest revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the Everest support system within the platform.

About – Everest

Welcome to Everest

Everest is a modern and innovative social networking platform created to bring people closer together in the digital world. It is a place where users can connect, communicate, share ideas, and express themselves freely. Everest is designed to make online interaction simple, enjoyable, and meaningful for everyone.

1. Our Platform

Everest allows users to create their own profiles and build their online identity. On the platform, people can share posts, upload photos and videos, and interact with others through likes, comments, and shares.

The platform is built to make communication easy and engaging so that users can stay connected with friends, family, and new people from around the world.

2. Our Mission

Our mission is to connect people from different cultures, places, and backgrounds through one simple platform. Everest aims to create a friendly and welcoming online space where everyone can share their thoughts, experiences, creativity, and moments without barriers.

3. What You Can Do

  • Creating and sharing posts with the community
  • Uploading photos and videos
  • Following friends and discovering new people
  • Liking, commenting, and sharing content
  • Expressing thoughts, ideas, and daily moments

4. Community

Everest focuses on building a positive and respectful online community. The platform encourages users to communicate kindly, support each other, and share meaningful content.

5. Our Vision

Our vision is to grow Everest into a global social networking platform where people from every corner of the world can easily connect, communicate, and share their stories.

6. Our Goal

Our goal is to build a simple, fast, and user-friendly social media platform that anyone can use easily.

Get Verified

Get Your Verification Badge

To receive a verification badge on Everest, your account must have at least 100k followers.

Verification is used to highlight notable and trusted accounts on the platform. Once your account reaches the required follower count, you may become eligible for badge review.

Important Notes

  • Your account should be active and authentic
  • Fake followers or misleading activity are not allowed
  • Your profile should have a name and profile photo
  • Badge eligibility may be reviewed manually
